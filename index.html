Our Story

From Passion to Sri Lanka's Premier Activewear Brand

Founded by fitness entrepreneur Joel Tillekeratne, Carnage began as an online store with a vision to create high-quality activewear that blends performance, comfort, and style.

Today, with our flagship store on Duplication Road and presence at Havelock City Mall, Carnage has become Sri Lanka's leading active and lifestyle clothing brand, trusted by fitness enthusiasts, athletes, and style-conscious individuals alike.
